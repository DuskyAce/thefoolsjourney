The Journey was a game made to give people a chance to go on life changing journeys, adventures of self-discovery, a chance to find themselves. The game would give them the power of self actualisation.But as with most tales a tragedy strikes.
A virus manages to slip its way inside The Journey, who gives it the same powers as a player. The virus kills the in-game holder of the game's controls and takes them for herself.The Journey North is a twisted mockery of what it once was. Queen Nortena now holds the reins of the unfinished game and seeks not to help her players, but keep them trapped, to use them to complete it.Unfortunate souls who stumble upon the game are not able to leave, instead getting stuck or molded to fit an unfilled role. They become just another part of the game.But... something has changed.

They were so burned out. They had done the right thing, gotten into a good university, studied hard (enough), and finally earned their degree. But all it led to was a dead end job working at a chain selling overpriced coffee and dry pastries.They thought that maybe being the hero was the solution to their ennui, but they ended up just as paralysed by the expectations they felt.Maybe it was for the best that they lost their memories for a while, as with them went those expectations…BACK

Wandering the world with nothing but a satchel full of potions, a magic broom, and a new feline friend, they finally begin to forge their own path.When faced with the injustices of the land, which a version of themself they could no longer recall would have felt helpless against, they decided to rebel. Known both as a wanted criminal, and as a benevolent healer, they start to come into themself. They begin to yearn for their lost memories less and less. Perhaps some small part of them does remember, but holds no love for their past self.It is no wonder then, when it is time for things to end, they continue to rebel. Surely it is better for all of them to stay? Surely the old world holds nothing for them. Alas, but they cannot live in fantasy forever. All they can do is bring the lessons they learned with them. And, perhaps, a feline friend…BACK

And so they did. They swallowed their fears and doubts, and forged their own path once again. Things weren’t always easy, and their relationships with the others had irreversibly changed, both for better and for worse. But they continued onwards regardless.
They had finally found their Strength.BACK
